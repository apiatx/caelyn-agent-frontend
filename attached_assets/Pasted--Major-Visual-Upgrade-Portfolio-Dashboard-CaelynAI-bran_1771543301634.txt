## Major Visual Upgrade: Portfolio Dashboard — CaelynAI brand theme

### Design Vision
The Portfolio Dashboard needs to match the CaelynAI brand identity. The brand is inspired by the CaelynAI logo — an ethereal ice-blue and white tribal fairy with circuit/tech elements on a deep black-blue background. The homepage already captures this with its deep navy gradients and blue/cyan accents. The Portfolio Dashboard currently looks like a generic dark dashboard. It needs to feel premium, mystical-tech, and distinctly CaelynAI.

### Color Palette (extract these from the homepage and logo):
PRIMARY BACKGROUND:     #0a0b1a (deep space navy — near-black with blue undertone)
SECONDARY BACKGROUND:   #0f1129 (slightly lighter navy for cards)
CARD BACKGROUND:        #141632 (cards, panels, table container)
CARD BORDER:            #1e2148 with subtle glow: box-shadow: 0 0 15px rgba(59, 130, 246, 0.05)
ACCENT GRADIENT:        linear-gradient(135deg, #6366f1, #3b82f6, #06b6d4) — purple → blue → cyan (the CaelynAI signature)
ICE BLUE GLOW:          #38bdf8 (for highlights, active states, important numbers)
FROST WHITE:            #e2e8f0 (primary text)
MUTED TEXT:             #64748b (secondary/label text)
POSITIVE GREEN:         #10b981 (keep — P&L positive)
NEGATIVE RED:           #ef4444 (keep — P&L negative)
TABLE ROW HOVER:        rgba(99, 102, 241, 0.08) — subtle purple tint on hover
DIVIDER LINES:          #1e2148 (subtle, not harsh)

### What "success" looks like:
The Portfolio Dashboard should look like it was designed by the same person who created the CaelynAI logo. Deep space navy backgrounds, ice-blue accents that glow subtly, purple-to-cyan gradient on interactive elements, frosted white text, and an overall feeling of premium tech-mystical elegance. When you switch between the CaelynAI homepage and the Portfolio Dashboard, they should feel like two rooms in the same mansion — not two different apartments.
